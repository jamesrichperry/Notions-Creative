Where We Are

Set in the Heart of Queenstown

Our workshops take place at Folk & Art Studio on Country Lane — nestled in the vibrant creative district of central Queenstown, surrounded by the stunning Southern Alps.

Whether you're a local or visiting this incredible corner of New Zealand, a Notions Creative workshop is a brilliant way to slow down, make something with your hands, and connect.

Your Host

Hey, I'm Emily

I run weekly macrame and creative workshops here in Queenstown, and I absolutely love teaching people to knot!

My workshops are designed to be fun, relaxed, and genuinely skill-building — whether you've never picked up a rope before or you're already hooked on macrame, there's something here for you.

I believe creativity is for everyone. Come along, make something beautiful, and leave feeling proud of what you've made.
